Work
  • UMN Mathematical Biology Researcher (2021)

    I built a mathematical model to analyze colorectal cancer cell growth in CRC-on-Chip Microfluidic devices operated by the Mumenthaler Lab. I was advised by Jasmine Foo, working with her and Shannon Mumenthaler.

    On a technical level, I dug into multitype stochastic branching processes, implementing in Python and MatLab with a maximum likeliestimation algorithm to optimize parameters, allowing me to determine the cells' rates of birth, death, and phenotypic switching.

    I collaborated with the Mumenthaler Lab in designing experiments to ensure the data they collected was maximally informative and encapsulated the important biological aspects of their setup.

  • UMN Mathematics Instructor (2019-2021)

    I designed and taught courses in calculus 1, calculus 2, and linear algebra for University of Minnesota Undergraduates.

    As one of the first points of contact of students with the university, I also helped them learn to navigate the university system.

    Due to the wide range of studetn educational backgrounds, I had to make sure everyone could learn and understand the material while not leaving any students bored.

    In my teaching philosophy, I place particular emphasis on the fact that, due to our range of knowledge and interests, aspects of mathematics that come quickly to some may be less comprehensible to others, and vice versa.

  • Competition Math Instructor (2015-2019)

    I have taught at IDEA MATH and Star League summer and winter math camps to help talented, highly motivated high school students succeed at mathematics competitions.

    I prepared and presented materials in geometry and combonatorics, designed challenging quizzes, and provided stylistic feedback to hone their math presentation skills. In my presentations, I contextualized complex problem solving techniques so the students could more easily recognize when these methods could be applicable vs. when to try something different.

    Many alumni of this program, including my students, have gone on to win national math competitions.
